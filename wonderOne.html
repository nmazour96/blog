Christ Redeemer

AD 1931

Fact #1

Located in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.

Fact #2

It is 98 feet tall (not including the 26 foot pedestal), and the arms stretch to 92 feet wide.

Fact #3

The statue weighs approximately 635 tons.

Fact #4

It is the 5th largest statue of Jesus.

Fact #5

The cost in 1931 was approximately $250,000 US. Today that would roughly be the equivalent of $3.2 million US.
